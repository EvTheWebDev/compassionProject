Making a Difference, One Life at a Time.

The Compassion Project Inc. is dedicated to empowering communities and transforming lives through sustainable programs and compassionate outreach. We believe in fostering self-sufficiency and ensuring everyone has access to basic necessities and opportunities for growth.

From providing emergency relief to long-term educational support, our work touches thousands of individuals each year. We strive to create a world where every person feels valued and has the resources to thrive.

How We Help

Thanksgiving Meals

On Thanksgiving, no one in our community should go without a warm meal. We prepare and share hot holiday meals for families and individuals facing hardship- because everyone deserves comfort, connection, and a full plate on this day of gratitude.

Learn More

Compassion Hardship Relief Program

Hardship Relief puts cash help where it matters fast. Refer a neighbor or apply yourself. If selected, we mobilize support and provide a lump-sum grant for urgent needs.

Learn More

The Kindness Ripple

A small act can spark a big change. Through the Kindness Ripple Program, we hand out small tokens of kindness, inviting each recipient to keep the ripple going by “paying it forward.” Together, we’re creating waves of kindness across our community.

Learn More

The Leanna Legacy Fund

Created in honor of Leanna Chroman, whose boundless compassion laid the foundation for The Compassion Project who are currently employed in the food and beverage industry who are facing hardship. Whether it’s an unexpected setback or a difficult season, this fund provides meaningful support to help those who serve our community every day.
