The Leanna Legacy Fund

Celebrating a Heart that Inspires Compassion

About the Fund

The Leanna Legacy Fund was established to celebrate Leanna Chroman, a compassionate leader whose generosity and empathy have inspired countless acts of kindness, including the creation of The Compassion Project Inc. Through this fund, we honor her ongoing example of service by supporting those she has always championed: individuals in the hospitality industry who are facing hardship or striving to make a difference in their communities.

Our Mission

To carry forward Leanna’s spirit of compassion by offering financial assistance, encouragement, and recognition to hospitality professionals who demonstrate resilience, kindness, and care for others in times of need.

Who Can Apply

Applicants Must:

  • Be currently employed in the hospitality industry (hotels, restaurants, catering, event services, lodging, or related fields)
  • Be experiencing financial or personal hardship or actively supporting others in need
  • Live or work within The Compassion Project’s Central Indiana service area
  • Apply directly or be nominated by an employer, coworker, or community member

How it Works

  1. Apply or Nominate: Individuals can apply or be nominated online, sharing their story and how assistance could make a difference.
  2. Review & Selection: The Leanna Legacy Committee reviews applications quarterly, focusing on compassion, integrity, and demonstrated need.
  3. Award: Recipients receive a financial grant or essential support to help alleviate hardship and encourage continued community impact.
  4. Recognition: With consent, recipients’ stories are shared through The Compassion Project under the hashtag #TheLeannaLegacy, highlighting the ripple effect of compassion in action.

Funding the Legacy

The fund is sustained through:

  • Donations made in Leanna's honor
  • Annual fundraisers
  • Partnerships with the hospitality community
  • Ongoing support from “Legacy Circle” donors

Continuing Her Impact

Leanna continues to lead by example, showing that one person’s compassion can inspire an entire movement. Through this fund, her kindness will keep uplifting those who serve others every day.
