Rooted Relief Express

Bringing compassion on wheels.

Overview

Rooted Relief Express is our mobile pantry and closet designed to meet people where they are. This traveling truck will deliver essential items, food, hygiene products, clothing, and care packages, directly to individuals and families across Central Indiana who are experiencing hardship.

Purpose

Many people in need face transportation and accessibility challenges that prevent them from reaching traditional resource centers. Rooted Relief Express removes those barriers by bringing support directly to neighborhoods, shelters, and community spaces. Our goal is to provide not only essentials, but also dignity, connection, and hope.

Program Status:

  • Status: In Progress
  • Goal: Purchase and outfit the truck

How It Works

  1. Stocking the Truck: The truck will be stocked with food, hygiene items, clothing, and care packages.
  2. Scheduled Routes: Scheduled routes and pop-up events will take place throughout Central Indiana in partnership with local organizations, shelters, and community centers.
  3. Distribution: Volunteers will help sort, load, and distribute items, ensuring every stop serves those who need it most.
