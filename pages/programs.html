Thanksgiving Meals

Leanna said it perfectly: "This one small act has such a ripple effect. When families sit down together and share a meal, they form bonds and lasting core memories that carry into their adulthood. It's so much more than just food. It's a connection, and that's long-lasting."

Compassion Hardship Relief Program

When hardship hits close to home, your compassion helps us respond. Through community giving, we provide financial and essential support to individuals and families facing sudden challenges, offering relief, hope, and a reminder that kindness makes a difference.

The Kindness Ripple

A small act can spark a big change. Through the Kindness Ripple Program, we hand out small tokens of kindness, inviting each recipient to keep the ripple going by “paying it forward.” Together, we’re creating waves of kindness across our community.

The RTC Fund: Restoration Through Compassion

Created in honor of Leanna Chroman, whose boundless compassion laid the foundation for The Compassion Project. Named after her father, Ronald Theriault Chroman, and aimed at those who are currently employed in the food and beverage industry facing hardship.

Rooted Relief Express

Coming soon, our mobile pantry and closet will bring compassion on wheels, stocked with food, clothing, and hygiene essentials to reach individuals and families facing hardship. Rooted Relief Express will remove barriers, restore dignity, and ensure that no neighbor is left behind.
